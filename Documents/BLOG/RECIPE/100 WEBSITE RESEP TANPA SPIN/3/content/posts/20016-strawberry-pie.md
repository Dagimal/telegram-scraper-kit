- "3 Tbsp. strawberry Jell-O 1 small box for 2 pies"
- "1 c. sugar"
- "1 c. boiling water"
- "1 pt. strawberries, halved"
- "1 graham cracker pie crust"
- "Cool Whip optional"
directions:
- "Mix first 3 ingredients in saucepan."
- "Add boiling water and boil until mixture thickens and clears."
- "Let cool."
- "Add strawberry halves in pie shell."
- "Pour cooled Jell-O mixture over strawberries and refrigerate until congealed, about 3 hours."
- "Slice and top with Cool Whip, if desired."
