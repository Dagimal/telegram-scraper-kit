- "3 bananas"
- "3 Tbsp. milk"
- "1 tsp. vanilla"
- "1 1/2 tsp. soda"
- "1 1/2 c. plain flour"
- "1 egg"
- "1/2 tsp. salt"
- "1/2 c. chopped nuts"
directions:
- "Blend oil, sugar and egg."
- "Add bananas, milk, flour, soda, salt and vanilla."
- "Beat slow, about 2 minutes."
- "Then add nuts. Pour in a greased and floured loaf pan."
- "Bake at 350u00b0 for about 1 hour and 15 minutes."
