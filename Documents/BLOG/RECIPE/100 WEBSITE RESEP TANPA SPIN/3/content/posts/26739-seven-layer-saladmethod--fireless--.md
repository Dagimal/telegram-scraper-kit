- "8 canned and sliced water chestnuts"
- "1/3 c. carrot shreds"
- "1 10 oz. pkg. frozen peas uncooked"
- "2 c. mayonnaise"
- "1 Tbsp. sugar"
- "1/2 lb. bacon, cooked and crumbled"
- "1/3 c. grated Romano cheese"

- "1 hard-cooked egg"

- "Garnish before serving with egg, bacon and tomato."
- "Do not toss."
