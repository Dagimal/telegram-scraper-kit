- "2 eggs"

- "Set aside. Mix flour, baking powder and salt together."
- "Beat eggs well, gradually adding sugar."
- "Blend in chocolate mixture and vanilla. Finally, add flour mixture."

- "Bake at 350u00b0 for 20 minutes."
