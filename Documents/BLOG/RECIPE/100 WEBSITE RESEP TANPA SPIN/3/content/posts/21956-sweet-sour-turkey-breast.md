ingredients:
- "1/2 lb. turkey breast, cubed"
- "soy sauce"
- "1/2 tsp. ginger"
- "1 Tbsp. cornstarch"
- "1/2 tsp. m.s.g. Accent, if desired"
- "2 to 3 carrots, sliced"
- "1 can pineapple chunks and juice"
- "1 green pepper"
- "1 or 2 bouillon cubes"
- "1/2 c. brown sugar"
- "1/2 c. vinegar"
- "2 Tbsp. cornstarch"
- "2 Tbsp. soy sauce"
- "1 tsp. powdered ginger or 1-inch ginger root, sliced"
directions:
- "Put turkey breast into small bowl."
- "Add soy sauce to moisten, ginger, cornstarch and m.s.g."
- "Marinate about 1/2 hour."
- "Brown turkey in a little oil."
- "Remove from pan."
- "Spoon off excess oil. Into pan, add brown sugar, vinegar, 2 tablespoons soy sauce, juice from pineapple and ginger root."
- "Bring to boil."
- "Turn down heat and simmer a minute or so."
- "Add cornstarch which has been dissolved in a little cold water."
- "Add sliced green pepper, carrots and browned meat."
- "Cook a couple of minutes."
- "Add pineapple chunks and heat through."
- "Garnish with chopped green onions."
- "Serve over rice."
