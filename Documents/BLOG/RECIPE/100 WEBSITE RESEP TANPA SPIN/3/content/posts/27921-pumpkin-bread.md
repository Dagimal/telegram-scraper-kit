- "1 tsp. cinnamon"
- "1 c. oil"
- "3 c. sugar"
- "1 1/2 tsp. salt"
- "1 tsp. nutmeg"
- "2 c. pumpkin"
directions:
- "In a large bowl, put flour, sugar, soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg."
- "Make a well and add oil and pumpkin."
- "Mix well."
- "Divide into 3 loaf pans and bake at 350u00b0 for 1 hour."
- "You may use a tube pan and add nuts or raisins."
- "Freezes well."
