ingredients:
- "1 c. sugar"
- "1 1/2 c. flour, sifted"
- "1/4 c. or 3 Tbsp. cocoa"
- "1 tsp. baking soda"
- "1/2 tsp. salt"
- "1 tsp. vanilla"
- "1 tsp. vinegar"
- "1/2 c. oil"
- "1 c. cold water"
directions:
- "Sift together in ungreased pan the first 5 ingredients."
- "Make 3 impressions in the mixture."
- "Add the vanilla and vinegar in each of the impressions; in the other one, add the cooking oil."
- "Pour the cold water over all and mix with a fork until all is mixed well. Bake in pan that you mix the cake in."
- "Bake at 350u00b0 for 30 minutes. That is why they call it crazy cake."
