- "Bring to a boil, then cook 3 minutes."
- "In a mixing bowl, combine egg whites, cream of tartar and salt."
- "Beat into peaks."
- "Slowly add syrup, beating all the while."
- "Beat 5 minutes more."
- "Add vanilla and blend."
