- "Press in 8-inch pie pan."
- "Saute onions in butter until onions are clear."
- "Spoon into crust."
- "Combine eggs, milk, salt and pepper."
- "Pour over onions. Sprinkle with grated cheese."
- "Bake at 350u00b0 for 30 minutes."
