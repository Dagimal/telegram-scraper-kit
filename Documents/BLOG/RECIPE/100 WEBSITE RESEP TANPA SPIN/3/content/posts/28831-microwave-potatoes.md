- "1/4 c. minced onion"
- "1/2 tsp. garlic powder"
- "1/2 tsp. black pepper"
- "1/4 c. Parmesan cheese"
directions:
- "Place all ingredients, except cheese in a 3-quart microwave-safe dish with lid."
- "Microwave on High for 10 minutes. Stir; add cheese."
- "Stir again."
- "Cover and microwave on High for 10 minutes."
- "Stir again before serving."
