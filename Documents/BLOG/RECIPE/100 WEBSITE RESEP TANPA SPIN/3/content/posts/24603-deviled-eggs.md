ingredients:

- "1/8 tsp. salt"
- "dash of pepper"
- "1/4 tsp. dry mustard"
- "1 1/2 tsp. vinegar"
- "1/4 c. mayonnaise"
- "paprika"
directions:
- "Boil eggs; shell them."
- "Slice lengthwise."
- "Put yellow yolks in bowl."
- "Mash yolks until fine and crumbly."
- "Combine other ingredients with yolks."
- "Spoon into sliced egg whites. Sprinkle tops with paprika."
- "Refrigerate until ready to use."
