- "Mix cornstarch and 1 cup sugar."
- "Add lemon juice."
- "Beat in egg yolks, one at a time."
- "Mix milk in slowly."
- "Place in double boiler."
- "Add butter; cook until thickened."
- "Pour into baked pie shell."
- "Beat egg whites; add 3 tablespoons sugar; put on pie and brown."
