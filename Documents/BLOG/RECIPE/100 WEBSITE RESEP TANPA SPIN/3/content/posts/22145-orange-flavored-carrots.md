ingredients:
- "3 c. carrots, sliced"
- "1/8 tsp. salt"
- "2 Tbsp. frozen orange juice concentrate"
- "1 tsp. lemon juice"
- "2 Tbsp. onion, chopped"
- "1/2 c. boiling water"
directions:
- "Add carrots, onion and salt to boiling water."
- "Cover and simmer gently until carrots are tender, about 20 minutes."
- "Drain, but save 2 tablespoons of the cooking liquid."
- "Add orange juice concentrate, lemon juice and the cooking liquid you saved to the vegetables."
- "Stir gently."
- "Heat again until vegetables are hot."
