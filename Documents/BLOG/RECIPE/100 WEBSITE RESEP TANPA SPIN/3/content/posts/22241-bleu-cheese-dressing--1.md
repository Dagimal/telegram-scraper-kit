ingredients:
- "2 eggs, coddled for 1 minute"
- "1 c. olive oil approximately"
- "1/2 tsp. white pepper"
- "1 tsp. salt"
- "1 Tbsp. lemon juice"
- "1 Tbsp. tarragon vinegar or regular vinegar"
- "6 oz. veined cheese Blue, Roquefort or Stilton"
directions:
- "Coddle eggs in simmering not boiling water for exactly 60 seconds."
- "Put eggs in blender and blend for a few seconds, then slowly add half of the olive oil while blending."
- "Add the remaining ingredients, except for the rest of the oil, and blend until the cheese is chunked to the size desired."
- "Slowly add the rest of the oil while blending."
- "If the dressing starts getting too thick, stop adding the oil before the full cup is in."
