ingredients:
- "2 c. whole milk"
- "1 1/2 c. sugar"
- "1 oz. sq. unsweetened chocolate"
- "1 Tbsp. butter"
- "3 Tbsp. cornstarch"
- "1/2 c. whole milk"
- "3 extra large egg yolks"
- "1 tsp. vanilla"
- "1 c. miniature marshmallows"
- "1 9-inch cooked pastry pie shell"
- "whipped cream"
directions:
- "In a large heavy pan, bring 2 cups milk, sugar, butter and chocolate to a boil."
- "Remove from heat."
- "Whisk together cornstarch, remaining milk, egg yolks and vanilla."
- "Off the heat, add this to cooked mixture, stirring constantly."
- "Return to heat, adjusted to low."
- "Mix with wire whisk until thick and smooth."
- "Stir in marshmallows and continue stirring until melted."
- "Pour into cooked pie shell and refrigerate."
- "Pat wax paper on the pie while refrigerating to avoid cracking."
- "Top with whipped cream before serving."
- "Serves 6."
