- "1/2 tsp. salt"
- "2 c. pumpkin"
- "1 2/3 c. evaporated milk"
- "1 egg, slightly beaten"
- "2 9-inch pie crusts"
- "1/2 tsp. cinnamon"
- "1/2 tsp. nutmeg"
- "1/2 tsp. ginger"
directions:
- "Preheat oven at 375u00b0."
- "Stir in brown sugar, flour, spice and salt."
- "Mix pumpkin, milk and eggs."
- "Mix cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger."
- "Then mix all together until smooth."
- "Pour into pie shells and bake for 40 to 45 minutes."
