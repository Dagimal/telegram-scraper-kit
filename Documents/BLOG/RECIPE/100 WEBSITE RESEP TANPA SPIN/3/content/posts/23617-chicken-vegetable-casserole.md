- "1 16 oz. pkg. frozen California Blend vegetables"
- "4 oz. Cheddar cheese approximately 1 c., shredded"
- "1 Tbsp. chopped onion I use more like 1 chopped onion"
- "1/4 to 1/2 c. slivered almonds or croutons"
