ingredients:
- "1 3/4 c. sugar"
- "3 eggs"
- "1 c. cooking oil"
- "2 c. all-purpose flour"
- "1 tsp. soda"
- "1 tsp. cinnamon"
- "1 tsp. salt"
- "4 to 5 cooking apples tart, diced"
- "1 c. walnuts, chopped"
directions:
- "Blend together with wooden spoon the eggs, sugar and oil until oil is blended."
- "Sift flour, soda, cinnamon and salt."
- "Stir with spoon into egg mixture."
- "Do not overbeat."
- "Fold in diced apples and nuts."
- "Grease a 9 x 13-inch pan lightly."
- "Bake at 350u00b0 for 1 hour or until cake leaves side of pan."
- "When cold, sprinkle with powdered sugar."
