ingredients:
- "1 lb. ground beef"
- "1 c. soft bread crumbs"
- "1 egg, slightly beaten"
- "2 Tbsp. minced onion"
- "2 Tbsp. milk"
- "1 clove garlic, minced"
- "1/2 tsp. salt"
- "1/8 tsp. pepper"
- "1 Tbsp. vegetable oil"
- "2/3 c. chili sauce"
- "2/3 c. red currant or grape jelly"
directions:
- "Combine the first 8 ingredients; form into 40 bite size meatballs, using a full teaspoon for each."
- "Brown meatballs lightly in oil."
- "Cover; simmer for 5 minutes."
- "Drain excess fat. Combine chili sauce and jelly; pour over meatballs."
- "Heat, stirring occasionally, until jelly is melted."
- "Simmer for 10 to 12 minutes, until sauce has thickened."
- "Baste occasionally."
