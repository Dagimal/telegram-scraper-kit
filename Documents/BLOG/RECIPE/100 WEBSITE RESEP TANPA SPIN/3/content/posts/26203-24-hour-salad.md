- "1 c. sugar"

- "Add sugar to juice from pineapple and bring to boil."
- "Pour over gelatine; let it cool."
- "Mix pineapple, cream cheese and gelatine mixture."
- "Fold in whipped cream and nuts."
- "Let stand overnight."
