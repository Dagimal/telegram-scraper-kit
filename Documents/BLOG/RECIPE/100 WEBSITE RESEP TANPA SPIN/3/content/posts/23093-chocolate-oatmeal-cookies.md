- "1/2 c. peanut butter"

- "Combine cocoa, sugar, milk, salt and margarine."
- "Heat slowly; bring to a boil."
- "Boil for 1 minute."
- "Remove from heat, stir in peanut butter and vanilla."
- "Stir in oatmeal by spoonfuls."
- "Drop on waxed paper and let stand 25 minutes."
