- "Add unflavored gelatin."
- "Stir and add 1/2 cup cold water."
- "Spray cookie sheet with Pam."
- "Pour gelatin mixture on cookie sheet."
- "Refrigerate until set."
- "Cut into shapes with cookie cutters."
- "Eat with fingers."
