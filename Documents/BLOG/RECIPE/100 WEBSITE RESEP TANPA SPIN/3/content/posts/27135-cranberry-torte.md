ingredients:
- "1 lb. vanilla wafers"
- "1/4 lb. butter"
- "1 1/2 c. confectioners sugar"
- "1 egg, beaten"
- "1 c. ground, raw apples, peeled"
- "1 c. ground, raw cranberries"
- "1 c. drained crushed pineapple"
- "1 c. granulated sugar"
- "1 c. heavy cream, whipped"
directions:
- "Grind wafers to fine crumbs."
- "Spread half of crumbs in a 9 x 13-inch pan."
- "Cream butter and sugar."
- "Add egg and beat until creamy."
- "Spread creamed mixture over crumbs to edge of pan. Combine fruits and granulated sugar."
- "Let stand 20 minutes. Spread over creamed mixture."
- "Cover fruit with whipped cream."
- "Top with rest of vanilla wafer crumbs."
- "Chill 12 to 24 hours."
- "Serves 24."
