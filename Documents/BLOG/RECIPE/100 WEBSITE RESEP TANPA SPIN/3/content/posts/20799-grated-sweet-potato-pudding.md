ingredients:
- "3 lb. sweet potatoes or yams"
- "3 c. sugar"
- "4 eggs"
- "2 c. milk"
- "1 tsp. nutmeg"
- "dash of salt"
- "1/2 lb. soft butter"
directions:
- "Wash, peel and grate potatoes into a large pot."
- "In a large bowl, cream sugar, butter, salt and nutmeg."
- "Mix into pot with potatoes."
- "Combine in another bowl the milk and eggs, then add to dry ingredients."
- "Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is firm with a candied appearance."
- "Pour mixture into a 4-quart casserole and cook at 400u00b0 until browned."
- "May be served hot with a meal or cold as a dessert."
