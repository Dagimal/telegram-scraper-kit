- "3 Tbsp. Hersheys cocoa"

- "Melt margarine."
- "Combine eggs, sugar and vanilla flavoring with margarine. Gradually add flour and cocoa. Add 1/2 cup pecans to mixture. Pour mixture into 9 x 11-inch greased pan."
