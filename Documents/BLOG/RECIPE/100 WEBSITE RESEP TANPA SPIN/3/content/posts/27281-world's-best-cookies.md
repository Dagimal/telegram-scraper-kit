- "Add egg."

- "Sift together the flour, soda, cream of tartar and salt."
- "Add to creamed mixture, stirring well."
- "Add corn flakes."
- "Roll in 1-inch balls."
- "Mash with fork and bake 10 to 12 minutes at 350u00b0."
