ingredients:
- "1 c. buttermilk"
- "1/2 c. butter"
- "2 Tbsp. light corn syrup"
- "1 tsp. baking soda"
- "2 c. sugar"
- "1 c. chopped nuts"
- "1 Tbsp. vanilla"
directions:
- "Put first 5 ingredients in saucepan."
- "Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar is dissolved."
- "Cook until a soft ball is formed when dropped in cup of cold water."
- "Remove from heat and cool until lukewarm."
- "Beat until mixture loses its gloss. Stir in nuts and vanilla."
- "Pour in buttered 8-inch pan."
- "Cut into squares when firm but still warm."
