- "3 Tbsp. cornstarch"
- "2 Tbsp. lemon juice"
- "1 8 oz. Cool Whip"
- "1 cooked pie crust or graham cracker crust"
directions:
- "Mash half of peaches."
- "Add sugar, cornstarch and lemon juice. Cook until thick and clear."
- "Set aside to cool."
- "Slice remaining peaches and fold into cooled mixture."
- "Spoon into pie crust."
- "Top with Cool Whip."
- "Chill."
