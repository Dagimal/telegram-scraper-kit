- "Wash, peel, halve and pit peaches."
- "Add to syrup and heat 5 minutes."
- "Pack fruit and syrup into hot jars, leaving a 1/2-inch headspace."
- "Adjust lids."
- "Process in boiling water bath 20 minutes."
- "Makes 3 pints."
