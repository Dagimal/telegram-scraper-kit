ingredients:
- "1 3 oz. pkg. jello, any flavor"
- "2/3 c. boiling water"
- "2 c. ice cubes"
- "fruit optional"
- "1 8 oz. Cool Whip"
- "1 graham cracker crust"
directions:
- "Dissolve jello completely in boiling water."
- "Add ice cubes and stir until jello is thickened, about 2 to 3 minutes."
- "Remove any unmelted ice."
- "Using wire whisk, blend in Cool Whip; then whip until smooth."
- "Fold in fruit, if desired."
- "Spoon into crust. Chill 2 hours."
- "Strawberry jello and strawberries are great."
