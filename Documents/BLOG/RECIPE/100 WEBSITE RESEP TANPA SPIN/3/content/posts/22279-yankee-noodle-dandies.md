- "Melt chocolate and butterscotch morsels in top of double boiler over hot water."
- "Remove from heat; quickly stir in noodles and peanuts until evenly coated."
- "Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper."
- "Chill."
- "Store in refrigerator until ready to serve."
