- "1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg"
- "2 Tbsp. butter"
- "2 Tbsp. parsley"

- "Mix with remaining ingredients, except butter and parsley."
- "Transfer to an oiled 8 x 8 x 2-inch baking dish."
- "Dot with the butter."
