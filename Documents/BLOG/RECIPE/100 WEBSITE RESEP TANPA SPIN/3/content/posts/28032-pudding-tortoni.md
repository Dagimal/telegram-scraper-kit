ingredients:
- "1 small pkg. vanilla or coconut pudding not instant"
- "1 1/4 c. milk"
- "1/3 c. sugar"
- "1 tsp. vanilla or almond extract"
- "1 c. heavy cream"
- "1/3 c. toasted coconut and/or ground nuts"
directions:
- "Combine pudding mix, milk and sugar in saucepan."
- "Stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a full boil."
- "Remove from heat, cover with waxed paper and chill."
- "Then beat pudding; add extract. Whip cream and fold into pudding."
- "Pour into paper cups or muffin tins."
- "Sprinkle with toasted coconut and top with maraschino cherries."
- "Freeze until firm and eat directly from freezer. Serves 12."
