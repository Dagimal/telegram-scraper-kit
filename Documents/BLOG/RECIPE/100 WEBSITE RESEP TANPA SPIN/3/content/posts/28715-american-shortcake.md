- "2 c. flour"
- "2 Tbsp. sugar"
- "3 tsp. baking powder"
- "1/2 tsp. salt"
- "1 tsp. grated orange rind"
- "1/3 c. shortening"
- "3/4 c. milk"
- "sweetened whipped cream or frozen whipped topping"
directions:
- "Mix berries with sugar; set aside."
- "Mix dry ingredients with rind and cut in shortening until crumbly."
- "Stir in milk just until blended."
- "Turn out on lightly floured surface and knead 10 to 12 times."
- "Pat out to 1/2-inch thickness and cut into 3-inch circles. Place 1-inch apart on ungreased cookie sheet and bake at 450u00b0 until golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes."
- "Split and fill with whipped cream and berries."
