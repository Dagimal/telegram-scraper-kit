- "6 oz. cherry jello"

- "1 Tbsp. lemon juice, drained"
- "1 c. Pepsi"

- "Bring water and sugar to boil."
- "Remove from heat."
- "Stir in cherry pie filling."
- "Return to boil."
- "Remove from heat. Thoroughly stir in cherry jello."
- "Add pineapple, lemon juice and Pepsi."
- "Chill until set in 9 x 13-inch pan."
