- "3/4 to 1 c. sugar"
- "1/2 c. white vinegar"

- "Mix salt and water and pour over cucumber and onion; let stand 2 hours."
- "Drain dont rinse."
- "Mix sugar and white vinegar."
- "Stir until all the sugar is dissolved."
- "Pour over cucumber and onion. Pack in containers and put in freezer."
