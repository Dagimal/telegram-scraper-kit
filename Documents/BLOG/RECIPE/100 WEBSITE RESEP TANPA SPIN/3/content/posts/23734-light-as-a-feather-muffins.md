- "1/2 tsp. salt"
- "1 c. milk"
directions:
- "Heat oven to 375u00b0."
- "Grease muffin cups."
- "Mix sugar and shortening well."
- "Blend in egg."
- "Sift dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture alternately with milk."
- "Fill muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake 20 to 23 minutes."
- "Serve hot."
- "Makes 14 to 16 small muffins."
