- "Heat to boiling."
- "Boil for 5 minutes."
- "Remove from heat."
- "Add baking soda."
- "Pour over popped corn."
- "Spread on 2 cookie sheets."
- "Put into 200u00b0 oven for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes."
