ingredients:
- "1 loaf frozen white bread dough, thawed"
- "1 egg white, beaten with 1 tsp. water"
- "about 3 tsp. coarse salt"
directions:
- "Cover dough; let rise until doubled."
- "Divide in 12 equal pieces."
- "With lightly floured hands, roll each in 16-inch rope. Form in pretzel shape."
- "In large skillet, bring 2 inches water to boil."
- "Slip pretzels, a few at a time, into gently boiling water. Cook until pretzels rise to surface, about 45 seconds."
- "Remove with slotted spoon; drain on paper towel."
- "Place 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheet."
- "Brush with egg white mixture."
- "Sprinkle with salt."
- "Do not let pretzels rise."
- "Bake immediately in preheated 400u00b0 oven for 15 to 20 minutes until browned."
