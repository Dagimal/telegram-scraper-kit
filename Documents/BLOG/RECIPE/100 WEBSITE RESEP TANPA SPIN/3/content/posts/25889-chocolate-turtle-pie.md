ingredients:
- "2 c. all-purpose flour"
- "1 c. light brown sugar"
- "1/2 c. 1 stick unsalted butter, softened"
- "1 1/2 c. pecan halves"
- "1 c. prepared caramel sauce"
- "2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips"
directions:
- "Preheat oven to 350u00b0."
- "Spray a 9 to 10-inch pie pan with vegetable oil."
- "In bowl of food processor fitted with steel blade, combine flour, sugar and butter."
- "Pulse until mixture comes together in a ball."
- "Press into pie pan."
- "Prick a few times with a fork and place in oven for 5 minutes."
- "Sprinkle pecans onto partially cooked pie shell and pour caramel sauce over."
- "Return to oven and bake another 15 to 18 minutes or until crust is browned and filling is bubbling."
- "Remove from oven and sprinkle with chocolate chips."
- "When chips are melted, spread to form a topping. Chill, slice and serve."
- "Serves 8."
