- "Put in pan and bake at 350u00b0 for 30 minutes."
- "Pour off fat."
- "Mix peaches, sugar and ketchup and carefully pour over chestnuts."
- "Be careful not to cover picks or they become sticky."
- "Bake 1/2 hour more."
- "Eat or freeze."
