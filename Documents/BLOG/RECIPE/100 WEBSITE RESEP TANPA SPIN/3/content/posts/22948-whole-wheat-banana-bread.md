ingredients:
- "1/2 c. butter"
- "3/4 c. brown sugar"
- "1 egg"
- "1 c. unsifted whole wheat flour"
- "1/2 c. unsifted unbleached white flour"
- "1 tsp. baking soda"
- "3/4 tsp. salt"
- "1 1/4 c. mashed ripe bananas 2 large or 3 small"
- "1/4 c. yogurt"
directions:
- "Preheat oven to 350u00b0."
- "Cream butter and sugar together until light and creamy."
- "Beat in egg."
- "Sift together flour, baking soda and salt."
- "Combine the bananas and yogurt stirring just enough to mix."
- "Add dry ingredients alternately with banana mixture to butter mixture stirring just enough to combine well."
- "Turn into 9 x 5-inch oiled loaf pan."
- "Bake 50 to 60 minutes or until done. Cool in pan 10 minutes."
- "Remove from pan and finish cooling on a rack."
- "Yields 1 loaf."
