ingredients:
- "1 lb. soup beans"
- "1/3 c. brown sugar"
- "1 tsp. dry mustard"
- "1/2 c. ketchup"
- "1/2 c. bean broth"
- "6 strips bacon"
- "chopped onion optional"
directions:
- "Soak beans overnight."
- "Rinse beans thoroughly and drain."
- "Cover beans with water and cook until tender."
- "Drain beans and save water."
- "Set aside."
- "Mix brown sugar and mustard together."
- "Add ketchup and bean broth."
- "Blend together."
- "Grease a 9 x 13-inch pan. Add 1/2 beans and about 3 strips of bacon."
- "Then add remainder of beans and 3 more strips of bacon."
- "Add the ketchup sauce over entire pan."
- "Use a knife to mix around beans."
- "Add chopped onions, if desired."
- "Bake for 1 1/2 hours at 300u00b0."
