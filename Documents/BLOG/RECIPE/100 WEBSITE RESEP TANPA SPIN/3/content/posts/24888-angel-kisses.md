- "Bake at 350u00b0 for 18 to 20 minutes."
- "Remove cookies immediately."
- "If they stick to pan, return it to the oven."
- "Melt chocolate and shortening together; drizzle onto cookies."
- "Makes 3 1/2 dozen."
