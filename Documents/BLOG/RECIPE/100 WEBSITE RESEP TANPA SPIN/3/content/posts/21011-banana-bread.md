- "4 Tbsp. oleo"
- "1 egg"
- "1 1/2 c. flour"
- "1 tsp. salt"
- "4 medium bananas, mashed"
- "1 c. nuts"
directions:
- "Mix sugar, oleo and eggs and beat until smooth."
- "Add flour and salt."
- "Add mashed bananas and beat until well mixed."
- "Add nuts. Pour into well-greased loaf pan."
- "Bake at 350u00b0 until brown."
