- "Combine celery tops, 6 cups water, chicken, bay leaf, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper in a Dutch oven. Cover and bring to a boil."
- "Reduce heat; simmer 1 1/2 hours or until chicken is done."
