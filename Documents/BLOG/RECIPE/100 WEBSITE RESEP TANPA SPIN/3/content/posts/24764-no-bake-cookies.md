- "Bring to a boil the sugar, cocoa, oleo and milk."
- "Boil 2 minutes."
- "Shut off stove."
- "Add peanut butter, oatmeal and vanilla."
- "Stir well and drop by teaspoon on waxed paper."
- "Let cool for 20 minutes."
- "Peel off wax paper and eat cookies."
